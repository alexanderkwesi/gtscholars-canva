🎨 Canva for Kids

A Fun Guide to Making Amazing Designs!

🖥️ Step 1: Open Canva

1. Ask an adult to help you go to www.canva.com.

2. Sign in or create a free account with help from a parent or teacher.

🎨 Step 2: Start a New Project

1. On the home screen, click "Create a design".

2. Choose what you want to make — like a poster, card, or presentation.

3. A blank canvas will open!

🖼️ Step 3: Add Pictures and Text

Add Pictures:
- Click "Elements" or "Photos" on the left side.
- Search for anything you like: type in “cat” or “robot” and click your favorite!

Add Text:
- Click "Text".
- Choose a fun heading or type your own message.

✂️ Step 4: Remove Background from an Image

(This is a Pro feature — ask an adult if it’s not showing.)

  1. Click on the photo you added.
  2. At the top, click "Edit Photo".
  3. Choose "Background Remover".
  4. Wait a few seconds... Poof! The background is gone!

🎉 You can now place the person or object anywhere in your design!

🌟 Step 5: Make It Fancy!

  • Change colors
  • Add stickers or frames
  • Drag things to move them around
  • Click on shapes or drawings to decorate!

💾 Step 6: Save or Print

  1. Click "Share" or "Download" at the top.
  2. Choose PNG or PDF if you want to print.
  3. Ask an adult to help you save or print your masterpiece!

🧠 Tips for Young Designers

  • Use bright colors that make you happy 🎨
  • Don’t put too much stuff in one spot 🧼
  • Ask someone for feedback 👀
  • Have fun and try new things! 💡
